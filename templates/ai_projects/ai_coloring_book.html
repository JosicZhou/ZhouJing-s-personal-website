Project Overview

An innovative AI-powered coloring book application that generates personalized coloring pages based on user preferences, themes, and difficulty levels, designed for users of all ages.

This project showcases my expertise in user-centered design, accessibility considerations, and creating engaging experiences that blend creativity with AI technology.

Design Challenge

How might we create an AI coloring experience that is both engaging and accessible for users across different age groups and skill levels?

The challenge was to design an interface that could accommodate both children and adults, while ensuring the AI-generated content was appropriate, creative, and printable at high quality.

My Role & Process

AI Product Intern - UX Focus

1. User Research & Analysis

Conducted interviews with parents, teachers, and coloring enthusiasts to understand needs and pain points.

2. Competitive Analysis

Analyzed existing coloring apps and identified opportunities for AI-enhanced experiences.

3. Design & Prototyping

Created wireframes, prototypes, and conducted usability testing with target users.

4. AI Integration Planning

Collaborated with developers to ensure smooth AI image generation and processing.

Key Features Designed

Smart Theme Generator

AI-powered theme suggestion engine that creates personalized coloring page themes based on user interests and preferences.

Difficulty Adaptation

Dynamic complexity adjustment that automatically scales line detail and intricacy based on user's age and skill level.

Accessibility Features

High-contrast options, large print modes, and screen reader compatibility for users with visual impairments.

Creative Gallery

Community-driven gallery where users can share their completed artworks and discover inspiration from others.

User Research Insights

Parents valued educational content

85% of parent users wanted coloring pages that could teach concepts like shapes, letters, or nature.

Adults sought stress relief

Adult users primarily used coloring as a mindfulness and stress-reduction activity.

Print quality was crucial

90% of users wanted high-resolution, print-ready files for offline coloring.

Design Decisions & Rationale

Large, Touch-Friendly Interface

Designed large buttons and clear navigation to accommodate users of all ages, especially children and elderly users who may have difficulty with small interface elements.

Minimalist Color Palette

Used a calm, minimalist color scheme to avoid visual overwhelming and allow the generated coloring pages to be the main focus.

Progressive Disclosure

Implemented progressive disclosure to show advanced options only when needed, keeping the main interface simple for new users.
